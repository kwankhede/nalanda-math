Basic Operations are the foundation of all mathematics. They include Addition (+), Subtraction (‚àí), Multiplication (√ó), and Division (√∑).

Important Properties & Rules

1. Commutative Property (‡§ï‡•ç‡§∞‡§Æ‡§¨‡§¶‡§≤ ‡§ó‡•Å‡§£‡§ß‡§∞‡•ç‡§Æ)

Addition: a + b = b + a ‚Üí 5 + 3 = 3 + 5 = 8

Multiplication: a √ó b = b √ó a ‚Üí 4 √ó 7 = 7 √ó 4 = 28

Note: Does NOT work for subtraction or division!

2. Associative Property (‡§∏‡§æ‡§π‡§ö‡§∞‡•ç‡§Ø ‡§ó‡•Å‡§£‡§ß‡§∞‡•ç‡§Æ)

Addition: (a + b) + c = a + (b + c) ‚Üí (2 + 3) + 4 = 2 + (3 + 4) = 9

Multiplication: (a √ó b) √ó c = a √ó (b √ó c) ‚Üí (2 √ó 3) √ó 4 = 2 √ó (3 √ó 4) = 24

3. Distributive Property (‡§µ‡§ø‡§§‡§∞‡§£ ‡§ó‡•Å‡§£‡§ß‡§∞‡•ç‡§Æ)

a √ó (b + c) = (a √ó b) + (a √ó c)

Example: 5 √ó (3 + 2) = (5 √ó 3) + (5 √ó 2) = 15 + 10 = 25

Very useful for mental math!

4. Identity Elements

Addition: a + 0 = a (Zero is the additive identity)

Multiplication: a √ó 1 = a (One is the multiplicative identity)

5. Zero Property

Multiplication: a √ó 0 = 0 (Any number times zero is zero)

Division: 0 √∑ a = 0 (Zero divided by any number is zero)

Warning: a √∑ 0 is UNDEFINED!

6. Order of Operations ‚Äî BODMAS/PEMDAS

Brackets / Parentheses

Orders / Exponents

Division & Multiplication (left to right)

Addition & Subtraction (left to right)

Example: 3 + 4 √ó 2 = 3 + 8 = 11 (NOT 14!)

‚òÖ Quick Tips & Mental Math Strategies

  • ‚òÖ Doubles: 7 + 7 = 14, 8 √ó 8 = 64 (memorize these!)
  • ‚òÖ Adding 9: Add 10, then subtract 1 (47 + 9 = 47 + 10 ‚àí 1 = 56)
  • ‚òÖ Multiplying by 10: Add a zero (25 √ó 10 = 250)
  • ‚òÖ Multiplying by 5: Multiply by 10, then divide by 2
  • ‚òÖ Division check: Quotient √ó Divisor + Remainder = Dividend
  • ‚òÖ Inverse operations: Addition ‚Üî Subtraction, Multiplication ‚Üî Division
